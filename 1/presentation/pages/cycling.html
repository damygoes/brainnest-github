Cycling - as a hobby!

Cycling is an active hobby that can improve your fitness with minimal environmental impact. It encourages coordination and situational awareness, too. Cycling as a hobby can even become daily transportation, which saves money and eliminates emissions all while benefiting your health.

Why I love cycling

  • It provides a sense of freedom and autonomy.
  • It is a great way to experience familiar surroundings in a different way.
  • It reduces sign of aging and body fat.
  • It boosts your mood and leads to a healthier heart rate.
  • You can enjoy it alone or with a group of friends.

As great as cycling is, there can however be some shortcomings like:

  • The potential of being an expensive hobby or sport
  • A higher risk of injuries and safety.
  • Exposure to a wide range of weather conditions, which can be a limiting factor to some.
